About

The Weeknd, whose real name is Abel Tesfaye, is a Canadian singer, songwriter, and record producer. He is known for his distinctive falsetto vocals and dark, atmospheric sound.

Work

The Weeknd has released many successful albums like:

  • House of Balloons (2011)
  • Beauty Behind the Madness (2015)
  • Starboy (2016)
  • After Hours (2020)

Resume

  • 3 Grammy Awards
  • 9 Billboard Music Awards
  • 2 American Music Awards
  • 1 MTV Video Music Award
